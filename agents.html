PHOENIX

"Joke over, you are dead"

Duelist

Hailing from the U.K., Phoenix's star power shines through in his fighting style, igniting the battlefield with flash and flare. Whether he's got backup or not, he'll rush into a fight on his own terms.

Special Abilities

Curve Ball

EQUIP a flare orb that follows a curving path and detonates shortly after being thrown, blinding any players who see it. FIRE to curve the orb to the left, or use ALTERNATE FIRE to curve it to the right. The orb is perfect for disorienting opponents around corners or in tricky positions, providing a tactical edge when timed and placed effectively.
